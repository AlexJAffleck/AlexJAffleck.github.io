Hi, I'm
Alex Affleck

I graduate from Utah State University in Decemeber 2022. I am majoring in Information systems with an emphasis in Data Engineering. I am currently the Systems Adminstrator for the Shingo Institute.

I was born in Detroit, MI and moved to Idaho Falls, ID when I was four. I was raised there and graduated from Hillcrest High School with honors in 2017. After high school, I served a religious mission for two years in the Philippines. Upon returning, I began my studies at Utah State University. My hobbies are playing the piano, lifting weights, and cooking.

I worked for nearly a year at a woodshop making window shutters. There I learned to work both independently and with a team. I then was hired as the Systems Administrator for the Shingo Institute. I administer an instance of salesforce, cover basic IT responsiblites, and run a project called Insight. By collecting detailed information about different organization's workplace culture and outlook, I am able to make an interactable dashboard in Tableau that helps them to find areas of improvement and opportunities for business excellence.

I love learning and thinking. I often find myself getting lost in random searches for information as I try to satisfy my curiosity about everyday things. I find data to be fascinating - there is an overwhelming abundance of it yet a dire shortage of understanding. I enjoy solving complex problems and all of the insights and findings that accompany the process.
